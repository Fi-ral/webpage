Welcome Traveller..

You have entered a strange realm...

I am Fiiral, and this is my website for both personal projects and those that I work on either as a full collaborator or only tangentially. Most of these have to do with science fiction, both hard and soft, particularly concerning the nations and technologies within them, but with that interest directly coming from a series of nation roleplays that regularly reset, the themes and topics discussed here may vary wildly from article to article and at times show little coherence.

Nevertheless, I do hope you have some fun reading through them or even adapting some of it for your own writing, worldbuilding, painting or other creative works, or even joining one of the current projects. If that sounds interesting to you, they are all linked on the appropiate pages or you can join via this link. To apply, just send a quick writing sample into the channel and a Game Master will clue you in.

Alternatively, if you are here for the variety of calculators I made, I hope you can find what you need there. If you notice an error in one of its calculations, please do not hesitate to tell me either via Discord or by opening a pull request on the website's GitHub repository.
